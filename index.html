Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our plumbing services

What areas do you service?

We proudly serve the Texas Hill Country including Bandera, Boerne, Kerrville, Comfort, and surrounding areas. We provide both residential and commercial plumbing services throughout the region.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, Certified Texas Plumbing is fully licensed (License #M41194) and insured. We maintain all required certifications and stay up-to-date with the latest plumbing codes and regulations.

How quickly can you respond to service calls?

We prioritize prompt service and typically respond to service calls within 24-48 hours. For emergency situations, we offer expedited service whenever possible. Contact us directly for current availability.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we provide free estimates for new construction and remodeling projects. For repair work, we may charge a service call fee that can be applied toward the cost of repairs if you choose to proceed with our services.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, checks, and all major credit cards. For larger projects, we can discuss payment schedules that align with project milestones.
